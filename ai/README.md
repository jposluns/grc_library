# Artificial Intelligence Governance Library

This directory contains the enterprise Artificial Intelligence governance, risk, and assurance artefacts.
These documents define the structures, controls, evaluation methods, and lifecycle governance required to ensure safe, transparent, and responsible AI across the organization.

All AI materials follow:
- ISO 42001 Artificial Intelligence Management System
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework
- ISO 23894 AI Risk Management
- ISO 24028 Trustworthiness in AI
- COBIT 2025 Governance and Management Objectives
- CSA CCM v5
- Applicable regulatory and ethical obligations

All terminology is standardized in the Key Terms and Definitions Register, located at the root of the GRC library.

# Document Catalogue

The following list provides a high level overview of all AI artefacts and their intent.

---

## Frameworks

framework-ai-governance.md  
Defines the enterprise wide governance model for AI, including roles, responsibilities, committees, ethical principles, and oversight structures.

framework-ai-system-audit-and-certification.md  
Establishes the audit, assurance, and certification structure for AI systems, including evaluation levels, evidence requirements, and assessment methodology.

framework-ai-in-model-risk.md  
Defines in model risk domains, interpretability structures, adversarial evaluation methods, and lifecycle integration expectations.

---

## Standards

standard-ai-in-model-risk.md  
Mandatory controls and evidence requirements for interpretability, robustness, alignment, documentation, governance, and lifecycle management.

standard-ai-lifecycle-governance.md  
Defines requirements for model registration, approval, monitoring, retraining, documentation, and retirement across all AI lifecycle phases.

---

## Policies

policy-responsible-ai.md  
Defines enterprise obligations for ethical, lawful, and transparent AI usage, including fairness, accuracy, safety, and human oversight requirements.

---

## Procedures and Guidelines

procedure-ai-incident-handling.md  
Procedures for detection, escalation, containment, and remediation of AI related incidents, including model failures and adversarial events.

guidance-ai-usage-and-safety.md  
Provides advisory recommendations for staff using AI tools, assistants, development platforms, and generative systems.

---

## Mappings

mapping-ai-in-model-risk.md  
Maps the In Model Risk Framework and Standard to ISO, NIST, COBIT, and CSA CCM requirements for auditability and compliance traceability.

---

# How to Use This Directory

1. Start with frameworks to understand the high level governance and risk structures.
2. Apply standards to implement enforceable requirements during development, deployment, and operation.
3. Follow policies to align behavior with executive intent and enterprise principles.
4. Implement procedures for operationalization, incident handling, and day to day governance.
5. Use mappings to support audits, certifications, and external assurance activities.
