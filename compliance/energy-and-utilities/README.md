## Purpose

This directory contains energy-and-utilities-sector compliance content of the library: sector-wide regulatory requirements that apply to organizations operating in electricity, gas, water, district heating, or related utility infrastructure.

Content here is sector-conditional. Organizations outside the energy-and-utilities sector can omit this directory entirely without affecting the rest of the library's coherence.

---

## Applicability

This directory applies to organizations operating as any of the following:

- Electricity generation, transmission, and distribution operators.
- Natural-gas production, transmission, distribution, and storage operators.
- Renewable-energy generators (wind, solar, hydro, geothermal, biomass).
- Water and waste-water utilities.
- District heating and cooling operators.
- Smart-grid, smart-meter, and demand-response platform operators.
- Designated essential entities or critical infrastructure operators under jurisdiction-specific cyber regulations.

## Relationship to the main library

The main library is sector-agnostic and applies to all adopting organizations. This directory's content extends but does not contradict the main library. Where an energy-sector regulation requires specific control language, evidence formats, or operating cadences, the annex states those requirements explicitly. Where the regulation's controls coincide with main-library controls (especially ICS/OT-segregation, change management, incident response), the main-library control remains the implementation; the annex documents how the implementation maps to the regulation's expectations.

Adopting organizations operating in the energy-and-utilities sector consume:

1. The main library as the baseline.
2. The energy-and-utilities sector overview annex ([`annex-energy-and-utilities-sector-requirements.md`](annex-energy-and-utilities-sector-requirements.md)) for sector-wide requirements.
