## Purpose

This register defines the IT and information security roles and responsibilities for compliance with the BASC International Standard v6. BASC certification requires documented accountability for security controls. This register maps BASC control domains to responsible parties within IT, identifies the corresponding GRC library documents, and notes where IT ownership ends and operational ownership begins.

---

## Scope

This register covers only the IT and information security elements of BASC compliance. Physical security of facilities and operational cargo security are governed by the relevant operational functions and are outside the scope of this document.

## Ownership boundary

IT ownership covers the controls listed above. Physical facility security, cargo handling procedures, and operational partner screening are outside IT scope and are governed by the relevant operational and compliance functions.
