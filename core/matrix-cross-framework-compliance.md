Purpose

This matrix consolidates alignment between enterprise governance, risk, compliance, privacy, security, resilience, and artificial intelligence governance artefacts and the most widely recognized international frameworks. It supports audit traceability, certification readiness, external assurance activities, and integrated control validation across the GRC document corpus.

Scope

This matrix applies to all documents in the Enterprise GRC corpus. It identifies alignment with ISO and IEC standards, NIST frameworks, COBIT 2025 domains, and CSA Cloud Controls Matrix v5 control areas. The matrix supports internal and third-party audits, certification programs, annual compliance reviews, and cross-domain assurance planning.

Maintenance and Review

This matrix is reviewed annually or upon updates to international standards. Changes must be reflected in all governance documents, the Master Index, and the Continuous Improvement Register. Any new documents must be mapped upon creation to all applicable frameworks.
