## Purpose

This matrix provides authoritative cross-framework and cross-regulatory alignment for the Enterprise Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) documentation set. It enables traceability between GRC artefacts and referenced legal, regulatory, and standards-based obligations across privacy, cybersecurity, AI governance, digital resilience, ESG, and supply-chain security domains.

## Scope

This matrix applies to all CC0 governance documents within the GRC Library.  
It reflects how GRC artefacts align to obligations across global regulatory regimes and major international standards.  
It does not reproduce legal text and must be used in conjunction with the Global Regulatory Mapping Register.
