# Purpose

This register establishes authoritative, traceable alignment between global legal and regulatory obligations and the Enterprise Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) documentation set.  
It provides a consolidated view of how regulatory requirements are satisfied or operationalized through internal governance artefacts.

The register supports:

- Regulatory assurance during audits and certifications  
- Continuous monitoring of jurisdictional changes  
- Mapping obligations across privacy, cybersecurity, AI governance, trade integrity, and ESG domains  
- Evidence-based alignment and traceability  

**Referenced obligations must be interpreted alongside authoritative legal sources.**

---

# Scope

This register applies to every jurisdiction in which the organization operates or exchanges regulated, personal, financial, or customs data. It covers:

- Privacy and Data Protection  
- AI Governance and Accountability  
- Cybersecurity and Digital Resilience  
- Trade and Customs Integrity  
- ESG and Responsible Innovation  

The register connects regulatory obligations to internal GRC artefacts that implement or support compliance.
