# Purpose

This register provides traceable alignment between global regulatory obligations and the Enterprise Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) documentation set.  
It supports regulatory audits, external certifications, compliance reviews, and continuous monitoring of legal and jurisdictional changes.

The register enables:

- Systematic tracking of regional laws and regulations.  
- Identification of downstream GRC artefacts supporting compliance.  
- Evidence-based alignment for privacy, cybersecurity, AI governance, supply-chain integrity, and ESG obligations.  
- Enterprise-wide traceability and legal assurance.

---

# Scope

This register applies to every jurisdiction in which the organization conducts business or exchanges regulated, personal, or trade data.  

Coverage includes:

- Privacy and Data Protection Laws  
- AI Governance and Accountability Regulations  
- Cybersecurity and Digital Resilience Requirements  
- Trade, Customs, and Supply-Chain Security Mandates  
- Sustainability, ESG, and Responsible Technology Frameworks  

Referenced obligations are high-level and must be interpreted alongside authoritative legal sources and internal policies.

# 6. Update and Maintenance Protocol

- Reviewed bi-annually by Legal and Compliance under CISO oversight.  
- Regulatory changes must trigger an impact assessment logged in the Continuous Improvement Register.  
- Updates must be reflected in the Master Index.  
- Region-specific annexes are updated with local compliance leads.

---

# 7. Integration and Usage

- **Auditors** use this register for certification and legal conformity testing.  
- **Policy owners** use it when updating artefacts tied to regulatory clauses.  
- **Executives** use it for demonstrating compliance posture to regulators, partners, and ESG reporting bodies.
